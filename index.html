Our Story

Pretty Gurls Budget, PGB, is a safe and vibrant community for women to learn about finance, business and investing. Conversations about finance can be one of the most difficult to have. Most people want to reach their wealth goals, but often get overwhelmed on where to start. The finance industry seems to cater information in a way that doesnâ€™t include us. PGB was created with the intention to build a community where gurls interested in changing their financial future have all the tools and resources to be able to do so. At PGB, conversations over brunch and lipgloss turn into brainstorming sessions on budgeting and investing. At PGB, finance is for the gurls. We are all on the same journey and we want to invite other gurls to join us too!

Statistics We Want To Change

INVESTING GAP

Only 22% of women have invested money in the stock market compared to just 40% of men.

FUNDING INEQUALITY

In 2022 Women-founded startups received only 1.9% out of the $238.3 billion in venture capital.

CHILD MARRIAGE

Girls from the poorest 20 percent of families are twice as like to be forced into marriage before the age of 18.

FINANCIAL STRESS

Women are twice as likely as men to associate negative emotions with their finances.
